Our Products

A natural, simply made lip gloss consisting of few ingredients. We use a hydrating, restoring base

BENEFITS:

With our lip gloss, it's bye to chapped lips and hello to moisturized ones.

The gloss moisturizes and protects the lips from wind penetration and cold temperatures

(exactly what we need this time of year!), reducing inflamation and allowing the healing of the lip tissue.
