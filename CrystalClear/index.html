Welcome to our website, we are Crystal Clear.

-----

We are middle school entrepreneurs who joined together to create our company. Here at Crystal Clear, we create high-quality resin necklaces and boba keychains, each made by hand with care. The necklaces have various pressed flowers and gold flakes, and the boba keychains come in 3 colors with boba and ice cubes! The necklaces are great to wear for any occasions, and the keychains can be used to customise your belongings - Both make great gifts for your friends and family!
