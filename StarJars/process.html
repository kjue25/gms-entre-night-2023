Our Process!

We combined our creativity and goals to make our product, Starjars. We brainstormed the best ways to save our money and make an amazing product for you all. After disscusing resin alternitives, we landed on coating our jars with colorful glue and charms to help our Jars look the best they can. We are also going to use fairlylights and little objects that relate to certain themes that we will make with our jobs. we hope we have lighted up your life!

Our Ingredients

- one jar

- fairly lights

- a layer of glue on the jar

- a set of charms depending on theme
