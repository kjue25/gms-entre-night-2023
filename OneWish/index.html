What Is It, Actually?

Our company, One Wish, sells bracelets! But not just any bracelets... Our bracelets are wish bracelets! Why are they called "wish" bracelets, you might ask? When you tie this bracelet around your wrist you make a wish. When the bracelet eventually falls apart or breaks, then your wish might come true! It is a super fun, stylish, cool, cute, bracelet for anyone to wear!

Why buy it?

Our product makes a grat gift for anyone. We have many different versions/styles of it. Different colors, combos, charms, etc! Some of the combos we have are siblings, sisters, brothers, sister and brother, mom and daughter, father and son, mom and son, father and daughter, couples, best friends, and more! It is also a fun product for you to wear for yourself. Buying this product is also supporting a small buisness that is giving more than 20% of our profit to a charity, Make A Wish America

How We Came Up With The Idea

As a group we have thought long and hard about what to make. We wanted to make something people would buy and something that is not super basic. We had the idea of jewlery and we came up with many ways to make it uniqe. We eventually decided on a wish bracelet. Something that would be different but cute and cool. Something that can have many different styles for every type of person.
