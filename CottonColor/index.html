Welcome To Our Website!

We are Cotton Color. We make tie dye products, specifically bandanas, towels, and scrunchies. We include lots of color our products and we hope they add some color to your lives! Our products can be used in households and for clothing accesories. The towels are multi-purpose and can be used as hand or dish towels. They can also be washed easily. Our scrunchies can be worn in your hair or on your wrist. They make it easier to do things without hair in your face. Our bandanas can be used for head accesories, and fun games when camping!
