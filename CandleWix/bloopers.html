CandleWix - Bloopers

Doing our hard work to bring everyone the special candles they deserve, we made many mistakes, but mistakes made you grow. So we composed some of our not-so-great moments for your enjoyment. Remember, though, that no one is perfect, and even if we made mistakes, we got better along the way. We hope that some of this will make you laugh.

Among our many mistakes we: Almost set off the fire alarm, which WOULD have set off the sprinklers and destroyed our classmates products. Good thing that didn't happen. We also melted a plastic bag into shreds... Oops. We spilled wax all over the freezer in the Teachers Lounge (sorry!).
