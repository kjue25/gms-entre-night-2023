CandleWix - Process

1. Poke a hole through the bottom of the mold

2. Stick the wick through the hole and set it using chopsticks or popsicle sticks

3. Melt the wax

4. Add color and scent to the wax

5. Pour into a measuring cup

6. Pour into mold

7. Let out until it has hardened a bit (45ish minutes)

8. Put in fridge and let it finish hardening (1hour)

9. Demold

10. Package, and sell!
